MY RESUME

Since 2022, I've had the pleasure of working with a small but dynamic tissue company called CALLA for about a year and a half. Despite its size, CALLA has always had big ambitions, and in 2023, they made a bold move by venturing into a new market segment. This led to the creation of VANILA, a sister company specializing in food cleaning salt products. Being part of a small company meant that I wore many hats, and I was thrilled to be heavily involved in the inception of VANILA. From brainstorming sessions to product development meetings, I contributed my skills and expertise to help shape VANILA's identity and product line. It was an exhilarating experience to be part of a team that was building something from the ground up. Alongside my involvement in product creation, I also took on the responsibility of spearheading marketing campaigns for both CALLA and VANILA. Despite the limited resources and budget constraints of a small company, I embraced the challenge of creating impactful campaigns that would resonate with our target audience. From crafting compelling messaging to designing eye-catching visuals, I poured my heart and soul into each campaign, striving to effectively communicate the unique value propositions of our products. Working in a small company environment provided me with invaluable opportunities to take on diverse roles and responsibilities. It taught me the importance of adaptability, creativity, and resourcefulness in overcoming challenges and driving success. Being part of the growth journey of both CALLA and VANILA was not only professionally rewarding but also personally fulfilling. Looking back, I'm proud of the contributions I made to help elevate both companies and position them for future growth and success. The experience has reinforced my passion for entrepreneurship and my desire to continue making a meaningful impact in the business world.
